Tenant - Bookkeeping App for PG Owners

Tenant is a comprehensive property management and bookkeeping software designed specifically for PG (Paying Guest) owners and multi-unit property managers in India. Our SaaS platform streamlines tenant record management, utility tracking, rent collection, and daily expense logging—helping property owners reduce operational complexity and save time on administrative tasks.

  • Rent Payment Management: Track and record monthly rent payments, generate payment reminders, and maintain complete payment history for all tenants
  • Utility & Meter Tracking: Log electricity, water, and gas meter readings with automated billing calculations
  • Expense Management: Record maintenance costs, repairs, and daily operational expenses with categorization
  • Automated Reminders: Send rent due notifications and utility payment alerts to tenants automatically
  • Multi-Property Support: Manage multiple PG properties and buildings from a single dashboard
